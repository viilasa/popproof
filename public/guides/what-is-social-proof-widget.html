Types of Social Proof Widgets

Purchase Notifications

Show recent purchases with buyer location and timestamp. Example: "Sarah from New York purchased Premium Plan â€” 5 minutes ago." This is the most common type and works best on e-commerce product and checkout pages.

Live Visitor Count

Display how many people are currently viewing a page. Example: "47 people are viewing this page right now." Creates authentic urgency, especially effective on pricing and limited-offer pages.

Signup Alerts
